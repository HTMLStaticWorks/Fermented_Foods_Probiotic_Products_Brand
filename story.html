Our Origin & Sourcing Standards

Bred From Earth. Verified by Lab.

CULTURA was born out of a simple, clean obsession: connecting the ancient health-giving biology of slow-fermented foods with modern metagenomic transparency.

The Founders' Journey

Honoring the Microbial Forest

CULTURA was founded in 2022 by food engineers and gut health researchers Evelyn and Clara. Frustrated by the corporate supermarket shelf-space dominated by sterilized, vinegar-preserved, heated 'pickles' and sugar-laden probiotic capsules, they sought a return to raw metabolic integrity.

They partnered with organic family-owned cabbage farms in the valley and built a temperature-regulated fermentation library. The goal was to prove that traditional, slow wild-yeast and bacteria cultures could be scaled ethically while keeping every jar completely raw.

Today, we package over 15 distinct functional ferments, verifying the bacterial genome profile of each batch to ensure active colony cell survivability.

Production Protocols

Our Three Non-Negotiables

Regenerative Crops

We source only from organic farms that practice crop rotation and soil restoration, ensuring high micronutrient densities inside the vegetable cells.

Slow Small Batches

We do not accelerate fermentation using artificial chemical catalysts. We allow wild bacterial cohorts to mature naturally over 28 full days.

Carbon-Neutral Cold Chain

Every shipment is packed in carbon-compensated recycled board boxes using natural water-soluble plant starches for insulation liners.

Sourcing Integrity

Soil-to-Gut Accountability

We pay our growers 25% above standard market fair-trade rates to guarantee only premium crops are harvested. Our sea salt is sun-evaporated and unfiltered to preserve essential trace minerals (calcium, magnesium, potassium) which nourish Lactic Acid bacteria.

Every jar has a batch code printed on the bottom. You can scan it to read the exact harvest location, fermentation start date, laboratory CFU count log, and heavy-metal screening report.
