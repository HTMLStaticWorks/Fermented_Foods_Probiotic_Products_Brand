Chef's Probiotic Tip

To preserve the trillions of active probiotic cells in CULTURA products, **never cook or boil them directly**. Heat pasteurizes the bacteria and eliminates the enzyme benefits. Instead, add raw kimchi, kefir, and sauces as cold garnishes or stir them into warm plates just before serving!
