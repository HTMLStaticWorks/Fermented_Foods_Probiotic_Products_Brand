Quick FAQs

Do you ship internationally? +

Because our probiotic foods are unpasteurized and require temperature-stabilization, we currently only ship within the contiguous United States. We cannot ship internationally due to customs delays impacting product refrigeration.

How do I pause my recurring orders? +

You can pause your orders at any time. Simply log into your account dashboard, navigate to the 'Subscriptions' tab, select your plan, and click 'Pause Subscription'. You can also email or text us directly and we will take care of it for you.

What happens if my package arrives warm? +

Our insulated boxes are designed to keep the products cool for up to 48 hours in transit. If they arrive slightly warm, the bacteria are still perfectly safe and activeâ€”lactic acid is a natural preservative! Simply place them in the refrigerator immediately to restabilize them.
