Support Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

How are the products shipped? +

Our raw probiotic foods are shipped in fully insulated, biodegradable liners packed with cold gel packs. This keeps the live bacteria cold-stabilized during transit. We suggest putting them in the fridge as soon as they arrive.

Can I customize the items in my subscription box? +

Absolutely. Once you subscribe, you can log into your account dashboard at any time to swap products, add extra bottles of sauces, or increase/decrease quantities before your scheduled monthly shipping date.

Are there any contracts or cancellation fees? +

None. Our subscription plans are designed around maximum flexibility to fit your lifestyle. You can pause, skip, or cancel your orders at any time without paying any penalties or cancellation charges.

How long do the products last in the refrigerator? +

Because they are raw and active, they will continue to slowly ferment over time, even in the fridge. Our kimchi jars have a refrigerator shelf life of 6 months, kefir bottles last 3-4 weeks, and aged sauces stay fresh for up to a year.
