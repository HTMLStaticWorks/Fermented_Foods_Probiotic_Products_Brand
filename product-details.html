We practice traditional **Lacto-Fermentation** relying on native wild microflora. Cabbages are packed by hand into temperature-regulated fermentation tanks where air is excluded.

For 28 days, ambient lactic acid bacteria generate lactic acid, converting natural sugars, increasing active probiotic counts, and dropping pH levels to a stable 3.8. The result is a sharp, tangy crunch that is completely natural.

  • ✓ Microbiome Expansion: Diverse lactic-acid bacteria support digestive tract linings.
  • ✓ Digestive Enzymes: Raw active enzymes facilitate easier protein and carb absorption.
  • ✓ Rich Phytochemicals: Preserved vitamins A, C, and K directly nurture systemic cellular health.
